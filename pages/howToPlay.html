Card Battle Game

RULES

The game is a 2 player deck vs deck of cards game

the thing that decides if your card is the one

to win is its type, it basically plays like a four

way rock paper scissors *see diagram below

if the cards are of the same color or are of the same kind

the winner is decided based on the card value

J being the lowest and ace the highest

if they are of the same value as well then there's a coin flip

The twist: every deck of cards has a Joker which forces

no matter the other players card a coin flip.



The Strategy

The game is more than just luck just like counting cards

in blackjack, the way to win consistently is by understanding

the propabilites, trying to predict the opponent next card

by trying to understand what set deck he got and his play style.
